What GAO Found One in three older Americans dies with Alzheimer’s disease or a related dementia. A 2011 law created the National Alzheimer’s Project, which has been reauthorized through 2035. The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) has led the National Alzheimer’s Project, which encompasses a range of federal agencies and activities to address Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias.
